AI-Prowler Remote Access — Subscription Information
=====================================================

WHAT IS REMOTE ACCESS?
-----------------------
Remote Access allows you to connect your AI-Prowler knowledge base
to Claude.ai and other MCP-compatible AI tools from anywhere over the internet via HTTP with
your phone, tablet, or any computer. Without a subscription, AI-Prowler
works fully as a desktop application only.


SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
------------------
  Individual Plan       $50 initial connect fee, $10/month
    For personal use on an device with a web browser and internet connection.
    Includes: 1 subdomain, 1 active connection, email support.

  Small Business Plan   $50 initial connect fee, $30/month
    For teams of up to 5 users sharing one deployment.
    Includes: priority support, commercial use permitted.

  Enterprise Plan       Contact us for pricing
    For 6+ users, custom server deployments, and SLA support.

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER PAYMENT
---------------------------
  1. You email your Bearer token to david.vavro1@gmail.com
  2. Your subscription is activated in the registry within 24 hours
  3. Restart AI-Prowler's HTTP server — remote access is restored
  4. No other changes needed on your machine


GRACE PERIOD
------------
  If your subscription lapses, you have a 30-day grace period
  during which remote access continues to work with a warning
  banner shown on the login page. After 30 days, access is
  suspended until renewal.
